Air Slide Conveyor Systems


air slide conveyor systems will quickly and efficiently convey materials such as raw and finished cement, fly ash, and precipitator dust. The simple combination of air pressure and gravity allows powdered materials to flow like a fluid. Air slide conveyors are very efficient and quiet. And with no moving parts, these conveyors are also extremely cost effective and require very little maintenance.
