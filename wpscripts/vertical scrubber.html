The GREEN ID® vertical  Scrubber or gas filter is used in many industrial applications. Its uses include the cleaning of fumes from plating ( PCB Plating ), anodizing, steel pickling, printed circuit board plating, semi-conductor clean rooms, and corrosive odor control units.


TREATED POLLUTANTS:

Alkaline: NaOH, NaClO3, …

Acids: HCl, HF, HCN, HBr, H3PO4, …

Sulfur compounds: H2SO4, SO2, SO3, H2S, Mercaptans, …

               Nitrogen compounds: NH3, NO2, RNH2, …

                               Toxic gases: Cl2, BF3, SO3, …

The process involves establishing maximum contact between liquid and gas using a fine, intense spray with a high-efficiency exchange screen separating each section. The scrubber is equipped with a mist eliminator at the top, which removes most of the mist produced by the spraying. The scrubber is supplied complete, with spray circulation, circulation pump, automatic water supply and level controls.
